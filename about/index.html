About

I founded Jina AI in 2020 and have been leading it as CEO ever since. Previously, I led neural search at Tencent between 2018-2020 and worked on search and recommendation systems at Zalando Research between 2014-2018, where I created Fashion-MNIST (11,000+ citations). I got my Ph.D. from TU Munich(https://www.tum.de/en/) in 2014, focusing on adversarial and robust non-parametric Bayesian learning.

I’ve lived and worked in many places, including the San Francisco Bay Area, Berlin, Munich, Taipei, Beijing, and Shenzhen. I’m currently based in Mountain View.

Go West, young man!
